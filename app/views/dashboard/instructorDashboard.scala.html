Events

You can create events including a group of students. An event has three stages.
Stage 1: This stage has a multiple choice question


Stage 2: In this stage the students chat and discuss what should be the correct answer


Stage 3: In this stage, the students will get a chance to correct their answers and justify the same


Stage 4: In this stage, the students will face a followup question


Statistics

You can view the statistics of an event or an individual statistics by following appropriate
links on the left navigation bar


Live Feed

You can choose the event and get live feed of messages exchanged between students and intervene if necessary


Past Event Discussions

You can view the discussions which have happened in the past events


Event Summary
